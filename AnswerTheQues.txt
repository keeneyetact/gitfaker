What is a native app?
A native app is any app that can be downloaded and live on your mobile device, functioning as an independent program and often accessing different features of your device to work.

What is User Experience?
The user experience is how a user interacts with and experiences a product, system or service. It includes a person's perceptions of utility, ease of use, and efficiency.

What is User Experience (UX) Design? | IxDF
User experience (UX) design is the process design teams use to create products that provide meaningful and relevant experiences to users.


What Is the Difference Between eCommerce and WooCommerce? 
While eCommerce refers to any type of commercial transaction that is facilitated online, WooCommerce specifically refers to the WordPress plugin that enables an online store to be created on a WordPress website.
